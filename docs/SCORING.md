# Scoring Model

Each listing receives:

- Score (0–100)
- Confidence (%)

---

## Score Components

Deal
Usage
Risk
Speed

---

## Confidence

Confidence is derived from:

- Data completeness
- Verified seller status
- Model certainty
- Listing consistency

---

## Display Rules

On card view:
- Show score badge
- Show confidence
- Must remain inside container (no overflow)

On detail view:
- Full breakdown
- Explanation panel

---

## Risk Signals

- High hours
- High price vs category
- Unverified seller
- Incomplete fields

---

## Seller Listings

Private sellers default to:
- Higher risk weighting
- Lower baseline confidence
